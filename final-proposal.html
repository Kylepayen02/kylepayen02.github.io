For my final project, I want to create a website dedicated to the book All About Love by Bell Hooks. Through my site, I aim on highlighting three of my main takeaways from the book: we must have a clear definition of love; patriarchy (and other systems of dominaton) distorts our perception of true love; and love is not only feeling, but an action. The site I envision has a minimalistic design and incoporates animation and audio to enhance the user's experience.
