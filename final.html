All About Love

“To truly love we must learn to mix various ingredients - care, affection, recognition, respect, commitment, and trust, as well as honest and open communication.”

Our conception of love is ambiguous. Bell Hooks offers us an alternative: a clear definition of what love encompasses. This definiton provides us with the foundation to step closer to true love.

Justice

“In a society like ours, where children are denied full civil rights, it’s absolutely critical that parenting adults learn how to offer loving discipline. Setting boundaries and teaching children how to set boundaries for themselves prior to misbehavior is an essential part of loving parenting.”
Now Playing: Raury - Love is Not a Four Letter Word
Instead of thinking of love as just a feeling, we should also consider it as an intentional action. We have the ability to decide who we want to love and how we want to show up in their lives.
There are several tools needed to create systemic change within our current system, love being one of them. Transforming our relationship with love can be one step we can each individually make to spark a create a cultural change.
The ways in which we socialize young boys and girls is restricting. To transform our relationship with love, we must look at the ways in which gender roles prevents people from developing into their true selves.
